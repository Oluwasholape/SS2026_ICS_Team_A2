# SS2026_ICS_Team_A2
This repository contains the seminar work of Team A2 for the Industrial Communication Standards (ICS) Seminar at Hochschule Hamm-Lippstadt (HSHL),supervised by Prof. Dr.-Ing. Ali Hayek.
Our topic is Safety Communication Protocols in Industrial Systems, with a focus on how functional safety requirements are met over standard industrial networks. We examine protocols such as PROFIsafe and CIP Safety, analyze the principles of safe data transmission and discuss certification standards and reliability considerations under IEC 61784-3 and IEC 62061.
The goal of this paper is to provide a structured introduction to safety communication, illustrate a real-world application scenario, and critically compare the leading safety protocol solutions in terms of latency, redundancy, and certification overhead.
